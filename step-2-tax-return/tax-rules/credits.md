# Tax Credits

> ⚠️ **Verify all credit amounts and income limits** against official IRS sources. These change frequently.

## Nonrefundable vs. Refundable Credits

| Type | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **Nonrefundable** | Can reduce your tax to $0 but won't generate a refund beyond that |
| **Refundable** | Can reduce your tax below $0, resulting in a refund even if you owe no tax |
| **Partially refundable** | Has both nonrefundable and refundable components |

## Common Credits

### Child Tax Credit (Partially Refundable)
- **Amount**: Up to $X per qualifying child under 17 (VERIFY with IRS)
- **Income phase-out**: Begins at $200,000 (Single) / $400,000 (MFJ)
- **Refundable portion**: Additional Child Tax Credit (Schedule 8812)
- **Form**: Schedule 8812 → Form 1040 Line 19 (nonrefundable) and Line 28 (refundable)
- **Verify**: [IRS Child Tax Credit](https://www.irs.gov/credits-deductions/individuals/child-tax-credit)

### Earned Income Tax Credit — EITC (Refundable)
- **Eligibility**: Must have earned income, AGI below threshold
- **Amount**: Varies by filing status, number of qualifying children, and income
- **No children**: Small credit available
- **Form**: Form 1040 Line 27a, Schedule EIC
- **Verify**: [Publication 596](https://www.irs.gov/publications/p596)
- **IMPORTANT**: Not available if filing MFS

### American Opportunity Tax Credit — AOTC (Partially Refundable)
- **Amount**: Up to $2,500 per eligible student (first 4 years of post-secondary education)
- **Refundable portion**: 40% (up to $1,000) is refundable
- **Income phase-out**: MAGI $80,000-$90,000 (Single) / $160,000-$180,000 (MFJ)
- **Form**: Form 8863 → Form 1040 Line 29 (refundable) and Schedule 3 Line 2 (nonrefundable)
- **Verify**: [Publication 970](https://www.irs.gov/publications/p970)

### Lifetime Learning Credit (Nonrefundable)
- **Amount**: Up to $2,000 per return (20% of first $10,000 of expenses)
- **No limit on years**: Can claim for any post-secondary education
- **Income phase-out**: MAGI $80,000-$90,000 (Single) / $160,000-$180,000 (MFJ)
- **Form**: Form 8863 → Schedule 3 Line 2
- **Note**: Cannot claim both AOTC and LLC for same student
- **Verify**: [Publication 970](https://www.irs.gov/publications/p970)

### Foreign Tax Credit (Nonrefundable)
- **What**: Credit for taxes paid to a foreign country
- **Simple method**: If total foreign tax ≤ $300 ($600 MFJ), can claim directly on Schedule 3
- **Complex**: Use Form 1116 for larger amounts
- **Source**: 1099-INT Box 6, 1099-DIV Box 7
- **Form**: Form 1116 or Schedule 3 Line 1
- **Verify**: [Publication 514](https://www.irs.gov/publications/p514)

### Child and Dependent Care Credit (Nonrefundable)
- **What**: Credit for childcare expenses that allow you to work
- **Maximum expenses**: $3,000 for one child, $6,000 for two+
- **Credit rate**: 20%-35% of expenses (depends on AGI)
- **Form**: Form 2441 → Schedule 3 Line 2
- **Verify**: [Publication 503](https://www.irs.gov/publications/p503)

### Saver's Credit (Nonrefundable)
- **What**: Credit for contributions to retirement accounts
- **Amount**: 10%, 20%, or 50% of contributions up to $2,000 ($4,000 MFJ)
- **Income limits**: Low-to-moderate income (VERIFY thresholds)
- **Form**: Form 8880 → Schedule 3 Line 4
- **Verify**: [IRS Saver's Credit](https://www.irs.gov/retirement-plans/plan-participant-employee/retirement-savings-contributions-savers-credit)

### Residential Energy Credits (Nonrefundable)
- **What**: Credit for energy-efficient home improvements
- **Amount**: 30% of qualified expenses, up to annual limits
- **Form**: Form 5695 → Schedule 3 Line 5
- **Verify**: [IRS Energy Incentives](https://www.irs.gov/credits-deductions/energy-efficient-home-improvement-credit)

## Credits Flowchart

```
Form 1040 Line 19: Child tax credit (from Schedule 8812)
Form 1040 Line 20: Amount from Schedule 3, Line 8
    ├── Schedule 3, Line 1: Foreign tax credit
    ├── Schedule 3, Line 2: Education credits (8863)
    ├── Schedule 3, Line 4: Retirement savings (8880)
    ├── Schedule 3, Line 5: Energy credits (5695)
    ├── Schedule 3, Line 6: Other nonrefundable credits
    └── Schedule 3, Line 8: Total

Form 1040 Line 27: Earned income credit (EIC)
Form 1040 Line 28: Additional child tax credit (8812)
Form 1040 Line 29: American opportunity credit (8863)
```
