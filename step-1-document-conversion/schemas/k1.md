## Triggers

- Any K-1 received → **Schedule E, Part II** required
- Box 1 > 0 (partnership) with Box 14 > 0 → **Schedule SE** required (self-employment)
- Box 8 or Box 9a ≠ 0 → **Schedule D** required
- Box 10 or Box 9 (S-corp) ≠ 0 → **Form 4797** required
- Box 16 or Box 14 (S-corp) has foreign transactions → **Form 1116** may be required
- Box 20 Code Z or Box 17 Code V → **Form 8995** (QBI deduction)

## Important Notes

- **S Corporation K-1 does NOT generate self-employment tax** (unlike partnership K-1)
- K-1 income is reported whether or not distributions were actually received
- Distributions (Box 19 or Box 16) reduce basis but are generally NOT taxable unless they exceed basis
- **Basis limitation**: Losses cannot exceed the partner's/shareholder's basis in the entity
- **At-risk rules** (Form 6198) and **passive activity rules** (Form 8582) may further limit loss deductions
- The K-1 is one of the most complex tax documents — always review the attached supplemental schedules for code breakdowns
