## Key Fields for Tax Return

| 1099-MISC Box | Flows To | Notes |
|--------------|----------|-------|
| Box 1 (Rents) | Schedule E, Part I (rental income) | If rental real estate activity |
| Box 2 (Royalties) | Schedule E, Part I, Line 4 | Royalty income |
| Box 3 (Other income) | Schedule 1, Line 8z (other income) | Prizes, awards, gambling, etc. |
| Box 4 | Form 1040, Line 25b (federal withholding) | Always |
| Box 5 (Fishing boat proceeds) | Schedule C (fishing business) | Self-employment income |
| Box 6 (Medical payments) | Schedule C (if medical practice) | For medical/health care providers |
| Box 8 (Substitute payments) | Schedule 1, Line 8z | Not eligible for reduced dividend rates |
| Box 9 (Crop insurance) | Schedule F, Line 6a | Farm income |
| Box 10 (Attorney proceeds) | Attorney's income reporting | Reported by attorney |
| Box 12 (409A deferrals) | Informational | May trigger additional tax |
| Box 14 (Excess golden parachute) | Form 1040, additional 20% excise tax | Via Form 5329 or Schedule 2 |
| Box 15 (NQDC) | Form 1040, Line 1h or Schedule 1 | Additional 20% tax may apply |
| Box 16 | State tax return | State withholding |

## Triggers

- Box 1 > 0 (Rents) → **Schedule E** (unless actively running a rental business → Schedule C)
- Box 2 > 0 (Royalties) → **Schedule E, Part I**
- Box 3 > 0 (Other income) → **Schedule 1, Line 8z**
- Box 5 > 0 or Box 6 > 0 → **Schedule C + Schedule SE** (self-employment income)
- Box 9 > 0 (Crop insurance) → **Schedule F** (farm income)

## Important Notes

- **1099-MISC is NOT for nonemployee compensation** — that moved to 1099-NEC starting in tax year 2020
- Box 3 "Other income" is a catch-all — could be prizes, awards, punitive damages, or other taxable payments
- Rents in Box 1 may be subject to self-employment tax if the taxpayer is in the real estate trade or business
- Royalties in Box 2 may be subject to self-employment tax if received in the course of a trade or business
