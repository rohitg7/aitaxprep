## Reporting Categories (Box A-D)

Transactions are categorized for Form 8949:

| Category | Holding Period | Basis Reported to IRS | Form 8949 Box |
|----------|--------------|----------------------|---------------|
| A | Short-term (≤1 year) | Yes | Box A |
| B | Short-term (≤1 year) | No | Box B |
| D | Long-term (>1 year) | Yes | Box D |
| E | Long-term (>1 year) | No | Box E |

## Key Fields for Tax Return

| Field | Flows To |
|-------|----------|
| Each transaction | Form 8949, Part I (short-term) or Part II (long-term) |
| Totals by category | Schedule D, Lines 1a/1b/2/3 (short) or 8a/8b/9/10 (long) |
| Net short-term gain/loss | Schedule D, Line 7 |
| Net long-term gain/loss | Schedule D, Line 15 |
| Net capital gain/loss | Form 1040, Line 7 (via Schedule D Line 21) |

## Important Notes

- **Wash sales**: If `wash_sale_loss` > 0, the loss is disallowed and added to the replacement security's cost basis
- **Cost basis not reported**: If basis was NOT reported to the IRS, you must enter the correct basis on Form 8949
- **Holding period**: Short-term = acquired and sold within ≤365 days; Long-term = held >365 days
- **Capital loss limit**: Net capital losses are limited to $3,000 deduction ($1,500 if MFS)
