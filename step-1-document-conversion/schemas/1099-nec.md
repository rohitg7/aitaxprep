## Key Fields for Tax Return

| Box 5 | State tax return (state withholding) |

## Triggers

- Box 1 > 0 → **Schedule C required** (Profit or Loss From Business)
- Net Schedule C profit > 400.00 → **Schedule SE required** (Self-Employment Tax)
- Schedule SE tax → **Schedule 1** (50% SE tax deduction on Line 15)
- Box 1 > 0 → Consider **Form 8995** (Qualified Business Income Deduction, 20% of QBI)

## Important Notes

- **1099-NEC replaced 1099-MISC Box 7** for reporting nonemployee compensation starting in tax year 2020
- Multiple 1099-NECs from different payers can be combined on a single Schedule C if they relate to the same business activity
- Different business activities require separate Schedule C forms
- The recipient is responsible for paying both income tax AND self-employment tax (15.3% — VERIFY against IRS) on this income
- Business expenses are deducted on Schedule C to reduce the taxable amount
